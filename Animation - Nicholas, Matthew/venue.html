Venue

The Oracle Stadium is the Home Stadium for the Golden State Warriors, able to hold up to 19,596 people. The arena opened in 1966 and is the oldest arena in the NBA. Tickets can be bought on the Ticket Page under the "Animation" Tab. The address for the stadium is 7000 Coliseum Way, Oakland, CA 94621, USA.
